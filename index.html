SEO & Analytics Observation Page

Use this page to perform and record observations for Google Search Console and Analytics.

Task 1: Mobile Usability & Indexing Observation

Step 1: Open Google Search Console.

Step 2: Go to Experience > Mobile Usability.

Observation Point Details / Findings
Common Errors to Check
  • Text too small to read
  • Clickable elements too close together
  • Content wider than screen
Mobile-First Indexing Importance Google uses the mobile version of your site for ranking. If mobile usability is poor, your site's overall SEO rank will drop because Google prioritizes mobile-friendly content.

Task 2: Traffic Analysis & Voice Search Optimization

Step 1: Open Google Analytics.

Step 2: Go to Reports > Engagement > Pages and screens.

Observation Point Details / Action Plan
Top Viewed Pages Identify the URL with the highest "Views" in your Analytics table.
Voice Search Optimization (For Top Page)
  1. Conversational Tone: Rewrite headers as questions (e.g., "How to...").
  2. Short Answers: Provide direct answers within 40-50 words.
  3. FAQ Schema: Add a Frequently Asked Questions section.
  4. Local Intent: Use "near me" or specific location keywords if applicable.

Note: This page is designed for manual observation of SEO tools for your GitHub site.
